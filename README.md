# **Smart Fall Detection System for Elderly People**

This project presents the design of a **smart wearable fall detection system** developed to ensure the safety of elderly people. The device automatically detects sudden falls and sends alert messages to family members and doctors so that immediate help can be provided.

---

## **Project Description**

Falls are one of the major causes of injury and death among elderly people. Many senior citizens live alone or may not be able to call for help after an accident. This project aims to solve this problem by creating a **wearable fall detection device** that continuously monitors the userâ€™s movement.

When a sudden fall is detected, the system automatically sends an **emergency alert message** to predefined contacts such as family members and doctors. This allows faster response, timely medical support, and helps reduce the risk of serious injury or death.

---

## **Key Features**

* Wearable and lightweight design
* Automatic detection of sudden falls
* Instant alert messages to family members and doctors
* Real-time emergency notification
* Improves safety and independence of elderly people

---

## **Working of the System**

1. The wearable device continuously monitors the user's movement.
2. A sudden fall is detected using motion sensing.
3. The system identifies the event as an emergency.
4. Alert messages are sent immediately to registered contacts.
5. Family members or medical staff can respond quickly and provide help.
